  • The Future

I am beyond excited to start gSchool. This feels like my golden ticket. I have been trying hard to break into this industry for a while now, and with gSchool's help, I know I am going to finally reach my goal of becoming a software developer. Why is software development my ultimate career choice? Here are a few reasons:

  • Creativity
  • Autonomy
  • Endless opportunities for professional and personal growth
  • Job Security
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, intellectually stimulating environment
  • Work with smart and passionate people

After school, I want to work at a start-up in town for the next few years or longer. In the long term, I could see myself wanting to teach at a program like gSchool or work at a remote company. Also, I would love to get involved with activism groups for women in tech.
